These are the public ladder rates. There is a higher hierarchy above Gold — accessible by accelerated climb or direct invitation. Its terms are not published.

How rates are applied (from spec):

• Card sales % applies to each card's sale price ($20 Virtual / $90 Signature / $225 Premium).

• Card spend % applies to the referral's monthly card spend volume directly.

• Futures fee % applies to the Tria taker fee (0.05% of trading volume) — not to the volume itself.

• Indirect commission is paid to the ambassador exactly two hops upstream of the transacting user at locked rates of 10% / 0.05% / 5% — independent of that ambassador's own ladder tier.

Tier thresholds, rates, and active-referral definition from the approved Season 3 ambassador-tiers spec. Calculator does not project the higher hierarchy above Gold.
