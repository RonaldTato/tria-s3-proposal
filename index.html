2. Where ether.fi has real vulnerabilities

a. Payout opacity

No disclosed cadence, currency, minimum, or method. "See earnings live" is shown but no settlement mechanics are public. Major trust gap.

Tria Spec is explicit: weekly Wednesday claim, $100 minimum, Merkle pipeline, paid to Tria Account.

b. No anti-fraud / anti-farming language

A referral program at this scale without documented clawback or idempotency is exposed to multi-account farming.

Tria The points-referrals.md spec explicitly handles atomic write semantics, reversal symmetry, and ops clawback paths.

c. Cashback ceiling is very low

0.1%–0.3% is barely material on small spend. Even at Tier 4 + 25% physical card commission, the ceiling on a 500-referral pyramid is modest.

Tria Tier-gated card commission is 20–30% on card purchase + 0.05–0.25% on every transaction, ongoing.

d. Pure commission program — no engagement layer

No points, badges, boxes, or VIP cashback on the referrer's own activity. Users have no reason to use the product themselves except for marginal cashback.

Tria Cash + Points + VIP Cashback gives the referrer 3 reinforcing reasons to be active.

e. 2nd-degree revshare gated behind 50 refs

New ambassadors get 0% indirect until they hit a high bar.

Tria Indirect (5% trading / 10% card) is unlocked from day 1, gated only by the referee's active status.

f. Single-product scope (cards only)

Trading, staking, restaking, transfers — none of it pays affiliate commissions. Their ETH-restaking flywheel and their referral program don't interact.

Tria Captures card spend + card txn fees + futures trading volume + VIP cashback on personal trading.

4. Trading-first additions — where we beat them

ether.fi's strongest mechanics — the New Member Bonus, High Spender Bonus, and all-time + 90d combo — are templates that translate cleanly to trading volume. Trading volumes are an order of magnitude larger than card spending, so the same mechanics produce much bigger numbers and more frequent celebration moments.

4.1 Trading Milestone Bonuses

Pattern lifted from ether.fi's "High Spender Bonus" — applied to lifetime trading volume.

Ref's lifetime trading volumeOne-time bonus to referrer
$50K$25
$250K$100
$1M$500
$10M$2,500 + "Whale Captured" badge
$100M$25,000 + named recognition

Each milestone fires once per referee. Creates anticipation as you watch a ref grow. ether.fi structurally can't do this — card spend volumes never reach these scales.

4.2 First Big Trade Bonus

Pattern lifted from ether.fi's "New Member Bonus" — applied to a referee's first qualifying trade.

  • Referee places first ≥$10K trade → $10 instant bonus + 50 Tria Points to the referrer.
  • Currently we just flip an "active" flag silently. This monetizes the activation moment.

4.3 Network Volume Tier (NVT) — new ladder

Today we count referral count. ether.fi does the same. Add a parallel ladder based on cumulative network trading volume, separate from the count-based Bronze→Gold tiers.

NVTLifetime Network VolumeBoost to your trading commission rate
NVT-1$1M+0.5%
NVT-2$10M+1%
NVT-3$50M+2%
NVT-4$250M+3%

Rewards quality. One $50M whale ref = NVT-3 even with only 5 refs. Quantity-only ladders punish ambassadors who bring in fewer-but-bigger users — which is exactly the user type we want most.

4.4 All-time + 90-day display

Steal directly. Surface both numbers in the Tier Progression card:

Network Volume: $4.2M lifetime · $850K last 90 days
Network Refs: 47 all-time · 18 active now

Honors the long-tenured ambassador who has a quiet quarter. Avoids the "lost everything because your refs took a vacation" feeling.

4.5 Network Volume Cashback — the asymmetric advantage

The one ether.fi will never match. Currently VIP cashback only applies to your own trading volume. Add: an additional cashback rate on your card spend, scaled by network trading volume.

Network 30d trading volBonus cashback on YOUR card
$1M+0.5%
$10M+1%
$50M+2%
$250M+3%

Stacks card + trading rewards in a way ether.fi cannot, because they only do cards. Builds a flywheel: ambassadors who recruit traders use their own card more (because the cashback is now meaningful).

4.6 Whale Capture Bonus

  • When a ref becomes VIP-3+ (≥$30M / 30d trading), referrer gets $1,000 one-time bonus.
  • Permanent +5% indirect on that whale's trading fees (overrides the standard 5%).
  • "Whale Whisperer" badge.

Aligns recruitment incentive with quality, not bodies. ether.fi pays the same regardless of whether your ref is a $50/mo card user or a millionaire.

4.7 Network Streak Bonus

  • Network maintains ≥$100K weekly volume for 4 consecutive weeks → +50% to trading commission for the next week.
  • 8 weeks → +100%.
  • 12 weeks → +150% AND unlock "Sustained Whale Network" badge.

Rewards consistency, prevents one-off spikes.

4.8 Asset-class multiplier — push Decibel

Volume on Decibel (Tria's native exchange) earns 1.5× the trading commission of Hyperliquid. Routes recruitment energy toward the venue where Tria captures more margin.

4.9 Volume Leaderboards

  • Top 100 ambassadors by 30-day network volume — public leaderboard.
  • Top 10 get +0.1% commission boost the following month.

ether.fi has no leaderboard at all. Pure social pressure mechanic.

5. Recommended priority — ship these three first

To change the program shape with the smallest engineering surface, start with these three. Each is self-contained and addresses a distinct gap.

Priority 1

Network Volume Tier (NVT)

Solves the "only counts bodies" problem. Rewards quality over quantity. ether.fi can't replicate.

Priority 2

Network Volume Cashback

Creates the card+trading flywheel ether.fi structurally cannot match. Single biggest moat.

Priority 3

All-time / 90-day dual display

Cheap to ship, immediate trust signal. Pure UI work, no new ledger logic.

Items 4.1, 4.2, 4.6, 4.7, and 4.9 are all great follow-ups but require new payout flows, ops work, or leaderboard infrastructure. Suggest staging them after the first three land.
